🦷 Crowns

At INE, we take the time to design long-lasting crowns made just for you that function well, and look and feel natural with your smile. A crown is an artificial restoration that fits over the remaining part of a prepared tooth, making it strong and giving it the shape of a natural tooth. A crown is sometimes known as a 'cap'.

✨ Veneers

We craft bespoke veneers that enhance your smile with natural-looking, long-lasting results — designed to feel as good as they look. Our Porcelain Veneers are ideal for making changes to the shape and/or color of your teeth. You'll usually get a more natural, attractive result by placing several veneers rather than just one. If you have existing dental work or very misaligned teeth, you may require more extensive cosmetic treatment.

🦷 Implants

Dental Implants are biocompatible titanium posts that are placed in the jawbone to mimic the root of a missing tooth. They act as a strong base to hold in place a replacement tooth. A prosthetic work such as a crown or a bridge or a denture can be attached to the implant to restore oral function to your mouth.
